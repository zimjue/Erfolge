Privacy Policy – MyVideoSpace

Your privacy is important. The MyVideoSpace app helps you organize YouTube videos into playlists and track how often you watch them.

The app is designed to collect as little personal data as possible. Most information is stored locally on your device.

1. What data is processed?

The app stores information that you enter yourself, such as YouTube video links, playlists, and optional usage statistics related to your video collections.

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. iCloud Synchronization

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your data may optionally be synchronized between your Apple devices using Apple iCloud services.

We do not access, store, or process this data on external servers.

3. Tracking / Analytics / Advertising

The app does not use tracking technologies, analytics services, or advertising frameworks.

No personal data is collected for analytics or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services that collect personal data. System services provided by Apple may be used where technically required.

5. In-App Purchases or Subscriptions

The app may offer optional paid features or subscriptions. All payments are processed securely through Apple via the App Store.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time or uninstall the app. Because all data is stored locally on your device, removing the app will also remove the stored data.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact: juzi7@icloud.com
