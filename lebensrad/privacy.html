Privacy Policy – Life Wheel

Your privacy is important. The Life Wheel app is designed to help you reflect on different areas of your life and gain more clarity.

All content is stored locally on your device.

1. Data Processing

The app stores content that you enter yourself, such as ratings, reflections or notes.

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Tracking / Analytics

The app does not use tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

3. Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used to collect or process personal data.

4. In-App Subscription

The app offers an optional subscription for exporting your Life Wheel as a PDF. Payments are processed securely through Apple via the App Store.

5. Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time or uninstall the app to remove all stored information.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy please contact: juzi7@icloud.com
